About Me

Hy, This is Prabeen Bahinipati,, a passionate UI designer with a solid two-year journey in the field. My journey began with mastering the basics - HTML, CSS, and JavaScript - and has evolved into a comprehensive skill set that includes React and Next.js, allowing me to bring my designs to life.

One of my favorite tools in the creative arsenal is GSAP (GreenSock Animation Platform). It's my go-to for crafting animations that add that extra flair to web pages, making them not just visually appealing but engaging as well. The art of animation doesn't stop there; I also thrive in using Framer Motion, a React animation library, to ensure a seamless user experience.

But I'm not just about static designs; I love incorporating Particle.js to infuse a dynamic and interactive element into my projects. It's all about creating user experiences that not only look good but also feel good.

In a world where UI/UX is ever-evolving, I pride myself on staying ahead of the curve. My work is a reflection of my dedication to striking the perfect balance between aesthetics and functionality. I'm not just a UI designer; I'm a visionary who believes in making digital experiences memorable and impactful.
